# 📊 Customer Churn Intelligence System

## 🚀 Overview
The **Customer Churn Intelligence System** is an AI-powered solution designed to help telecom businesses predict customer churn and take proactive retention actions.

The system analyzes customer data—including demographics, service usage, billing patterns, and support interactions—to identify customers who are likely to leave.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Predict customer churn using machine learning models  
- Assign churn probability scores to each customer  
- Segment customers into **Low, Medium, and High-risk categories**  
- Identify key factors driving churn  
- Provide actionable business insights and retention strategies  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Programming Language:** Python  
- **Data Processing:** Pandas, NumPy  
- **Machine Learning:** Scikit-learn  
- **Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn  

---

## 🤖 Models Used
- Logistic Regression  
- Decision Tree  
- Random Forest *(Final Selected Model)*  

---

## 📈 Evaluation Metrics
- Confusion Matrix  
- F1-Score *(Primary metric due to class imbalance)*  
- 5-Fold Cross-Validation  

---

## 📊 Business Insights
- Customers with **low tenure** are more likely to churn  
- **High monthly charges** significantly increase churn probability  
- **Month-to-month contracts** show the highest churn rate  
- Lack of **technical support** leads to higher customer dissatisfaction  

---

## 🎯 Retention Strategies

### 🔴 High-Risk Customers
- Offer targeted discounts and retention deals  
- Provide priority customer support  
- Encourage migration to long-term contracts  

### 🟡 Medium-Risk Customers
- Run engagement and reactivation campaigns  
- Provide personalized plan recommendations  

### 🟢 Low-Risk Customers
- Upsell premium services  
- Introduce referral and loyalty programs
